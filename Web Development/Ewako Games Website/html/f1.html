F1 2018 is a racing video game and the tenth installment in the Formula One video game franchise developed and published by Codemasters. The game is based on the 2018 Formula One World Championship and includes all twenty-one circuits from the calendar and all twenty drivers and ten teams competing in the season. It was released on 24 August 2018 for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One.[1] An iOS version released October 18, 2018. An Android version is expected to be released later in 2018
